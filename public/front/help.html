Help Center

Getting Started

If you're new to our recommendation system, start by creating an account and rating a few movies. This will help our algorithm learn your preferences and provide personalized recommendations.

Account Settings

You can manage your account settings, including your email, password, and notification preferences, by clicking on the "Account" tab in the navigation menu.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with the recommendation system, try clearing your browser's cache and cookies, or restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.
